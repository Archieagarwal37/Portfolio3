A UI/UX Design Case Study

Problem Statement

Users trying to start or sustain a fitness routine commonly face these challenges:

Fragmented tools : Workouts, diet tracking, goals, and progress are often split across multiple apps.

Lack of personalization : Most fitness apps don't adapt to individual body types, goals, or fitness levels.

Low motivation : Without timely reminders, milestone tracking, or community support, users quickly lose consistency.

Complex interfaces : Many apps clutter the experience with too many metrics, unclear flows, or inaccessible language.

This often leads to low retention, poor habit formation, and fitness becoming a burden rather than a lifestyle.


Project Overview

YoursFit is an all-in-one fitness app designed to help users:

  • Set customized fitness goals (weight loss, muscle gain, flexibility, etc.)
  • Access beginner-to-pro level workout plans based on preferences and equipment availability
  • Track daily steps, calories, hydration, and mood
  • Get AI-generated suggestions and progress reminders
  • Join community challenges and track achievements

    • The design currently includes the entire app interface, with plans to extend it into a wearable integration for smart tracking.


    Design Approach

    Simplify fitness onboarding for first-time users.
    Create an adaptive fitness experience that evolves with user progress.
    Design clean, motivating interfaces that reduce user fatigue.
    Ensure accessibility and inclusivity across all demographics.
    Promote habit-building through visuals and subtle nudges


    Design Process

    1. User Research & Insights

    Conducted user interviews and surveys with 25+ fitness app users across varying fitness levels.

    Key Findings :

    70% feel overwhelmed by too many disconnected fitness features.
    65% drop out of routines within 3 weeks due to lack of personalization.
    58% said reminders and visualization of progress would improve motivation.
