Decora is a digital shopping platform built to transform that experience ‚Äî helping users discover and shop the perfect decor that brings life to every space.

Problem Statement

Users looking for home decor online often face:
Overwhelming product options with minimal guidance or personalization.
Lack of visual context, making it hard to picture how items fit together.
Tedious comparison process across platforms and styles.
Impersonal experiences, with generic recommendations and confusing layouts.
These challenges contribute to user drop-offs, cart abandonment, and low design satisfaction.


Project Overview

Decora is an e-commerce platform dedicated to curating and simplifying the decor shopping experience. The platform allows users to:.
Browse room-based collections (Living Room, Bedroom, Study, etc.).
Shop by style, color, and budget.
Visualize products with ‚Äúroom view‚Äù previews (coming soon).

The design scope covered the full website experience, from home to checkout, with scalability in mind for mobile and tablet interfaces.


Design Approach

Deliver a user-friendly, emotionally resonant shopping interface.
Help users visualize and coordinate decor pieces with confidence.
Reduce decision fatigue with intelligent filters and personalized ideas.
Create a calm, cohesive, and lifestyle-driven aesthetic.
Ensure a smooth, distraction-free path from discovery to purchase.


Design Process

1. Research & User Persona Building
Conducted online surveys and 1-on-1 interviews with 25+ decor shoppers.
Findings:
72% said they abandon carts due to lack of visual clarity or uncertainty in style fit.
65% want to shop by room type or mood, not just categories.
50% were influenced by curated collections or seasonal picks.
