Project: Ambuvians ‚Äì Accessible Healthcare Services

A UX/UI Website & App Design Case Study with Custom Illustrations


Introduction

Healthcare services need to be accessible, clear, and comforting across all digital platforms. Ambuvians is a comprehensive healthcare platform available as both a website and mobile app, designed to help users easily book ambulances, schedule health checkups, consult doctors online, and access facial scan services.
As the illustrator on this project, my role was to create custom drawn illustrations that add a human, approachable feel to the user experience‚Äîsoftening complex healthcare processes and building trust through visual storytelling.


Project Overview

Ambuvians offers users the ability to:
Book ambulances quickly with real-time tracking.
Consult doctors via video and chat.
Schedule health checkups and diagnostic tests.
Use facial scan technology for health monitoring.
Manage appointments, payments, and medical records.

The design team handled UX/UI flows and visual interface, while I developed a suite of hand-drawn illustrations that:
Guide users through key actions with clear, friendly visuals.
Soften medical and technical content to feel more personal and less intimidating.
Support accessibility with simple, recognizable iconography and scenes


Illustration Approach & Highlights

Warm, hand-drawn style using soft lines and gentle color fills to evoke calm and trust.
Illustrated ambulances, medical staff, health monitoring devices, and user scenarios with approachable expressions and dynamic poses.
Created step-by-step visual guides and onboarding illustrations to help users feel confident navigating services.
Designed responsive illustrations optimized for both web and mobile interfaces.
Developed a consistent illustration language that complements the platform‚Äôs modern UI and color scheme


Impact of Illustrations

Increased user engagement by making complex healthcare tasks feel simpler and friendlier.
Helped reduce user anxiety, particularly around emergency ambulance booking and health consultations.
Strengthened brand identity by creating a unique, memorable visual style.
Enhanced overall accessibility with clear, intuitive imagery supporting text and interactive elements


Reflections & Learnings

Working on Ambuvians allowed me to merge creativity with empathy‚Äîcrafting illustrations that not only beautify the product but also serve a meaningful role in making healthcare digital services more human and accessible.
Collaborating closely with UX/UI designers, I ensured that every illustration worked harmoniously within the flow, enhancing usability without distracting from critical¬†information.
